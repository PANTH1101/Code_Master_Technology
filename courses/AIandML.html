Course Overview

Course Title: AI & ML Masterclass

Course Description: This course is designed to equip learners with essential and advanced knowledge in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. You will explore data preprocessing, supervised and unsupervised learning, model building, and deployment. With hands-on projects, you'll learn to apply AI & ML techniques using Python and popular libraries like Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, and Pandas.

Target Audience: Suitable for students, developers, and tech enthusiasts aiming to build a strong foundation in AI and ML or transition into data science roles.

Prerequisites: Basic understanding of Python programming and high school-level mathematics will be helpful. No prior experience in AI/ML required.

Course Goals: By the end of this course, students will be able to:

  • Understand AI and ML fundamentals
  • Build and evaluate machine learning models
  • Apply real-world problem-solving using AI
  • Use Python libraries such as Scikit-learn, Pandas, and TensorFlow
  • Deploy and optimize AI solutions

Learning Format: Includes video lectures, coding assignments, quizzes, case studies, and project-based learning.

Learning Objectives

  • Grasp the fundamentals of AI and ML
  • Work with real datasets for training models
  • Understand supervised and unsupervised learning techniques
  • Build neural networks and deep learning models

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Introduction to AI & ML

Understand the basics of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, types of learning, and real-world applications across industries.

Module 2: Python for AI

Brush up your Python skills and explore libraries such as Numpy, Pandas, and Matplotlib for AI & ML development.

Module 3: Supervised and Unsupervised Learning

Learn to work with labeled and unlabeled data, train classification and regression models, and evaluate their performance.

Module 4: Neural Networks & Deep Learning

Explore the fundamentals of neural networks, deep learning with TensorFlow and Keras, and apply them to image and text data.

Key Technologies:

Python, Scikit-learn, Pandas, TensorFlow, Keras, Matplotlib, NumPy

These tools are critical for implementing, analyzing, and deploying machine learning models in real-world scenarios.
